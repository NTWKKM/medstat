MedStat: Professional Medical Statistics Tool

MedStat is a comprehensive, free-to-use medical statistics platform designed for researchers, clinicians, and biostatisticians. Our tool provides advanced analytical capabilities without the need for expensive software licenses.

Key Statistical Features:

  • Firth Logistic Regression: Handle small sample sizes and complete separation in clinical data.
  • Propensity Score Matching (PSM): Reduce bias in observational studies for better causal inference.
  • Survival Analysis: Professional Kaplan-Meier plotting and Cox Proportional Hazards Regression.
  • Biostatistics Dashboard: User-friendly interface for complex medical data analysis.

Designed for medical researchers worldwide. Fully secure, web-based, and no data is stored on our servers.
